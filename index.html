Juicers Reviewed

Mueller Austria

Mueller Juicer Ultra 1100W Power

The Mueller Austria Juicer is a budget-friendly affordable centrifugal juicer that boasts many of the features of high-end machines. It is quick and convenient. The juicer is made from durable stainless steel and BPA plastic. It has a vertical build that won’t take up too much space on your counter. It has a convenient 3-inch round chute so you can drop whole fruits and vegetables into it. It comes with a 34-ounce juice jug and a large 68-ounce pulp jug. The anti-slip silicone feet prevent the juicer from slipping when in operation. The juicer has a powerful 1100-watt dual speed motor. With speeds of up to 18,000 RPM, the juicer can extract as much as 16 ounces of juice in just 8 seconds. All removable parts are dishwasher safe and the juicer carries a two-year limited warranty.

Omega

Omega J8008C Juicer Extractor and Nutrition System

The Omega J8008C Juicer uses a cold-press technique when it comes to juicing, this technique has been well-documented and appreciated by most health advocates. It has a five setting adjustable end cap that will fully extract juices and nutrients. It automatically expels the pulp. The Omega works at speeds of under 80RPM. The low speed prevents oxidation and heat build-up, ensuring a top-quality juice that will last for up to three days in the refrigerator with no degradation or separation. It has a 200-watt, 120-volt commercial-grade motor. It also grinds coffee, makes nut butters and even extrudes pasta. Use it to make baby food, grind spices, shred herbs or make frozen deserts. The machine is quiet in operation.

NutriBullet

NutriBullet NBJ50100 Juicer

The NutriBullet NBJ50100 is a compact juicer designed for easy, everyday use. The NutriBullet Juicer features streamlined design, a 3” wide feed chute to cut down on juicing prep, and just a handful of easy-to-clean, dishwasher-safe parts, so you can get juiced and get on with your day. Dual Speed Control Two speeds give you full control over the softest or hardest fruits and veggies, so you can get the most out of your produce. The NutriBullet Juicer comes with an 800W Motor Base, 1.5L Pulp Basin with 3” wide feed chute, Stainless Steel Sieve, 27oz Juice Pitcher, Sealing pitcher lid with froth separator, Food Pusher, Cleaning Brush and User & Recipe Guide.
